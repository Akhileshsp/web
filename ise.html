The department of Information Science and Engineering came into existence in the year 2000.
It is headed by Dr. J. Prakash supported by a highly qualified and dedicated staff.

The Department is currently headed by Dr. J. PRAKASH BE, MS, PhD

Dr. J. PRAKASH has a rich experience of teaching and research for more than 20 years.

He has published 12+ research papers in the leading international journals and conference proceedings and authored several books in the areas of Digital Image Processing, Digital Signal Processing etc.

Realizing well in time that theinformation technology wave has become a phenomenon in itself over the past few years, the institute established a separate department to cater the ever-growing demand for professionals in this challenging field.

The department aims at cultivating thirst for knowledge, vigor and the vision to look beyond the evident in students.
The students are well versed with the exhaustive and thoroughly chosen curriculum, guided as they are by an experienced faculty.

ISESA

Department of Information Science, BIT in collaboration with Students and Staff presents ISE Student Association. The Student Association comprises of a team of five like-minded individuals. Two years of having a monotonous campus experience, we felt a need for change in our department. A change which was much needed.

With the support of the Head of Department and the staff of ISE we have decided to conduct activities and competitions within our department to encourage our fellow students to learn more. Our whole intention of forming an association was to make sure students come out of their comfort zone and expose themselves to change.

While doing so, we believe that these changes will help students during placements, help students become better individuals and have a positive impact on themselves and a society as a whole.

Concept of Houses :

Back when we were studying in schools we were divided into four different Houses.
Every competition a student wins, points would be added to the House the student belonged to. At the end of the academic year the House with most points would be declared a winner. By just conducting activities and competitions we felt that students might lose interest with time and that’s the reason we thought to implement the concept of Houses into the department.

The whole department is divided into four Houses.

They are: 1) Red House - Valour 2) Green House - Operose 3) Blue House - Mystic 4) Yellow House - Instinct


Purpose of Houses :

➼   Promote teacher-student, student-student interaction. Real education—the substantive development of intellect and character—depends upon sustained personal contact between students and teachers over the long term.
➼   Improve the campus life. This model allows us to renew our campus life and bring excitement in the daily routine of institution education.
➼   Genuine diversity - Education and experience that comes from exposure to students with different experiences of life, flourishes within “Houses” that are complete cross-sections of the demography to which they belong.
➼   The students can be part of something bigger than themselves - Bring in a sense of belonging .
➼   Student Welfare and Self Sustainability .
➼   The welfare of and is looked by students; themselves and allow them to :
          ☛ Develop soft skills .
          ☛ Obliterate hesitation to approach their classmates and faculty .
          ☛ Establish a strong formal and informal network of support.
